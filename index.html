About Me.

Kingsley Taylor

" I am passionate about helping individuals achieve their true potential, through coaching, mentoring and improving their leadership development."

There are many “coaches” in the market, however few can match my breadth of people development experience, my ability to help people push their comfort zone and my warm yet direct style.

Unusually for many of the coaches mentors and trainers in this field, I have actually been in senior roles ( CEO, Sales Director, Operations Director, Head of Sales and Operations) and this gives me the empathy, experience and the ability to understand the pressures of senior responsibility, which is why as well as coaching, clients ask me to mentor them.

I am currently working as a Leadership Development Associate with the CIPD ( Chartered Institute of Personnel Development ) helping Blue Chip organisations in the UK and Europe. I am a qualified Coach but I also Mentor as part of my service in helping the client develop.

Examples of my work in
the last two years

  • Coaching and Mentoring the CEO of the fastest growing outsourced people services company in the UK
  • Coaching and Mentoring the MD of one of the largest construction materials in Europe
  • Coaching and Mentoring the Sales Director of one of the largest tech firms in Europe
  • Building a bespoke leadership programme for the UK’s fastest growing natural food companies
  • Training over 30 different organisations in the UK and Europe for the CIPD
